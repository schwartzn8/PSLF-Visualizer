Desktop · Chrome / Firefox / Safari

🔖 Auto-Scraper Bookmarklet

The fastest method on desktop. The bookmarklet auto-pages through all your payment history on studentaid.gov and copies the data to your clipboard.

1
Drag the button below to your browser's bookmarks bar (or right-click → Bookmark this link)
2
Log in to studentaid.gov → navigate to your PSLF Payment History page — ⚠️ remove any active loan filters first
3
Click the bookmarklet — it pages through everything automatically (~15–30 sec) and copies JSON to your clipboard
4
Return here and paste below, then click Load Data
📊 PSLF Scraper ← drag to bookmarks bar
⚠️ Only works on the studentaid.gov PSLF payment history page.
Mobile · iOS Safari / Android Chrome

📱 Mobile Copy-Paste

No bookmarklet needed. On your phone, copy the visible text from the PSLF Payment History page — the tracker will parse it automatically. Works page by page.

1
On your phone, open studentaid.gov → log in → go to your PSLF Payment History page
2
Long-press on the page text → Select All → Copy
3
Open this tracker and paste below. Repeat for each page of payment history — the tracker merges them automatically
4
Click Parse & Load. The tracker detects the copied format automatically
💡 Tip: The page text parser looks for loan names, month/year periods, and status words (Qualifying, Non-Qualifying, Pending). It handles messy copied text gracefully. If a page doesn't parse well, try scrolling to load all rows first.
Desktop & Mobile · Any device
